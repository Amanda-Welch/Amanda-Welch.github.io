About Me

Amanda Welch graduated from the University of Sorocaba, in Brazil, in 2017 with a Bachelor’s Degree in Food and Nutrition. While in college, she earned a certificate in Gastronomy and worked as a nutrition intern taking care of each patient’s dietary needs at healthcare clinics and hospitals. And taking care of food safety and a team of cooks at a school kitchen and a big company’s industrial kitchen. In 2018 she moved to Utah. In 2019, she worked as an autonomous food and package deliverer, which taught her time management and to work without supervision. In 2021, she accepted a job at Honest Eatery in Salt Lake City as a cook/FOH where she quickly became an Assistant Manager due to her eagerness to learn and attention to detail. She is dedicated to the customer and passionate about creating a great experience that makes them come back. Amanda finds it incredibly motivating to use her behavior psychology interest to better her product/service. And she finds it inspiring to be part of a team made of unique minds working together in favor of making people’s day better. As a result of this experience, she gained skills in problem solving, handling money, leading a team and training new team members. In December 2022 she finished her Web and Graphic Design Certificate at Davis Technical College in Kaysville, Utah with 900 hours of experience in:
Design Principles, HTML, CSS and SASS, PHP, WordPress, SEO, Debugging, JavaScript (jQuery), UX/UI, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, Figma, InVision, Miro
Always being creative and out-of-the-box thinker, she decided that a career in the web design industry would be exciting since it involves two of the topics she loves the most: tech and arts. Her hobbies include hiking, biking, playing video games, board games, and all music related activities.
